Instructions

This game is for two players. Each player will take turns placing their ships on their grid. Drag the ship over the grid and drop it where you want it to go. Press 'R' to rotate them. Press the "Finished" button when you are done.
Once all ships have been placed, players will take turns attacking the other player's grid. To attack, click on the grid square you want to hit. If it is a hit, a red dot will appear. If it is a miss, a white one will display.
To win, you must destroy all your opponents ships before they destroy yours.
